TankView 360


TankView 360 is a prototype surveillance robot that I designed with a team of freshmen and sophmores for the 2018 Thales Arduino Challenge. We wanted to create a surveillance robot where users could see what the robot was seeing via a VR headset. Borrowing a Samsung Gear 360 camera The robot and Samsung 360 camera connect to a nearby WiFi network, where users can view a live feed of the robot's surroundings, and send commands to TankView 360 through a mobile app.
